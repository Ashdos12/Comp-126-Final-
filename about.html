About Us

Carolina Housing

Carolina Housing Blog is a community of UNC Chapel Hill students dedicated to expressing the very best that Carolina has to offer. Our team is comprised of a variety of majors, disciplines, and years, all invested in sharing the Carolina Experience. The blog started in 2019 as a classroom project after a group of students wanted to create a site that would give insight into the daily life at UNC, allowing students to connect through their shared Carolina experiences. After some months of planning and development, the project eventually became the blog it is today.

Our Mission

Carolina Housing Blog is a college life blog and was built with the UNC community in mind. We envision the blog functioning as a resource for students and alumni to connect, exchange information, and express themselves. The site is intended to be an informative resource for UNC students about life at the university but also as an entertaining, social outlet for students.

The topic of each post we host on our site is structured with this aim in mind, providing both entertainment and information to the Carolina community. From our posts on housing to food to activities and fun, students can get the inside scoop on current and trending topics that are pertinent to the very place where they live, work, and learn!
